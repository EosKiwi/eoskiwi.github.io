📜 Eos Kiwi Code of Conduct (English)

1. Legality & Compliance

  • We comply with all applicable laws and regulations in relevant jurisdictions.
  • Operations, fund usage, and data handling are conducted in a compliant manner.

2. Transparency

  • We disclose key infrastructure details (hardware, region, providers, contacts).
  • Any rewards or fund allocations are reported publicly on a regular basis.
  • Potential conflicts or affiliations are declared openly.

3. Community & Governance

  • We remain neutral—no vote manipulation, smear campaigns, or attacks.
  • We maintain open channels for community feedback and respond promptly.
  • We operate independently and are not controlled by a single entity.

4. Node Operations

  • Target uptime ≥ 99%; patch vulnerabilities and incidents in a timely manner.
  • No malicious forks; no insertion of invalid transactions.
  • Collaborate with the community and peers to maintain network stability.

5. Fairness & Conflicts

  • If multiple nodes are operated, we disclose and avoid unfair advantages.
  • No vote buying or opaque vote-trading agreements.
  • All statements are factual—no misleading claims.

6. Data & Privacy

  • Follow data-minimization; never misuse user data in APIs/services.
  • GDPR and other privacy standards are observed when applicable.

7. Oversight & Improvement

  • Open to community and third-party oversight and audits.
  • Violations will be disclosed with corrective actions (including suspension if needed).
  • This policy is living and will be updated as the network evolves.

📜 Eos Kiwi 行为准则(中文)

1. 合法性与合规

  • 遵守所在司法辖区及相关国际法律法规;
  • 节点运行/资金使用/数据处理均保持合规.

2. 透明度与公开性

  • 公开披露基础设施要点(硬件/地域/服务商/团队联络方式等);
  • 涉及奖励或资金用途的事项,将以报告或链上记录形式披露;
  • 如存在关联方或潜在利益关系,将如实声明.

3. 社区与治理

  • 保持中立,不操纵投票/不抹黑或攻击其他节点;
  • 提供公开沟通渠道,积极响应社区反馈;
  • 独立运营,不受单一组织或资本控制.

4. 节点行为

  • 目标可用性 ≥ 99%,及时修补安全漏洞与异常;
  • 不进行恶意分叉/不插入无效交易;
  • 在网络事件中与社区/基金会及同业协作以维护稳定.

5. 公平与利益冲突

  • 如运营多节点,将透明披露并避免不公平竞争;
  • 严禁买票或任何形式的不透明互投;
  • 对外宣传基于事实,不误导/不夸大.

6. 数据与隐私

  • 在 API/服务中遵守最小化收集原则,不滥用用户数据;
  • 涉及欧盟用户时遵循 GDPR 等国际隐私规范.

7. 监督与改进

  • 接受社区与第三方监督/审计;
  • 如有违规将主动说明并承担后果(含暂停相关职能);
  • 随网络发展持续修订本准则.
